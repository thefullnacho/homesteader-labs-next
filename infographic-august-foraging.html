IN SEASON DOES NOT MEAN SAFE.

Identify the whole plant, leaves, stem, habit, or it stays where it grew.

PEAK

BLACKBERRIES

The headline act, coast to coast. Beaded aggregate fruit on a thorny cane, the safest berry in North America. Pick DULL black. Shiny means 3 more days.

MOUNTAINS

HUCKLEBERRIES

August is THE month at elevation, plus late wild blueberries up north. Same family, same check: the 5-point crown on the blossom end. No crown, no deal.

COOK THEM

ELDERBERRIES

Turning purple-black now. Woody shrub, FLAT-TOPPED spray, opposite compound leaves. Raw = regret. And read the mixup warning below first.

SPIT THE PITS

WILD PLUMS & CHOKECHERRIES

Thicket fruit season in the middle of the map. Flesh is food, PITS ARE NOT: cherry-family pits carry cyanide compounds. Never crush them into jam.
